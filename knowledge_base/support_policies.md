# NovaCRM — Support Policies & Procedures

## Ticket Priority Levels

### P1 — Critical (Enterprise only)
- Definition: Platform is completely down or data loss is occurring
- Response time: 2 hours
- Resolution target: 8 hours
- Escalation: Automatic page to on-call engineering team

### P2 — High
- Definition: Major feature is broken, no workaround available
- Response time: 4 hours (Enterprise), 12 hours (Professional)
- Resolution target: 24 hours
- Examples: Cannot send emails, login failures, API returning errors

### P3 — Medium
- Definition: Feature is degraded but workaround exists
- Response time: 12 hours (Enterprise), 24 hours (Professional), 48 hours (Starter)
- Resolution target: 3 business days
- Examples: Slow dashboard loading, report generation delays, minor UI bugs

### P4 — Low
- Definition: General question, feature request, or cosmetic issue
- Response time: 24 hours (Enterprise), 48 hours (Professional/Starter)
- Resolution target: 5 business days
- Examples: How-to questions, feature suggestions, documentation corrections

## Refund Policy

- Monthly plans: Full refund if cancelled within 7 days of charge
- Annual plans: Pro-rated refund for remaining months if cancelled within 30 days
- No refund for partial months after the 7-day window
- Refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 5–10 business days

## Account Suspension

Accounts may be suspended for:
- Non-payment: 14 days past due → warning email. 30 days past due → suspension. 60 days → data deletion
- Terms of service violation: Immediate suspension with email notification
- Suspicious activity: Temporary lock until identity verification

To reactivate a suspended account, contact billing@novacrm.com with your account ID.

## Uptime SLA

- Enterprise plan: 99.9% uptime guaranteed (excludes scheduled maintenance)
- Scheduled maintenance window: Sundays, 2am–4am CT (advance notice given)
- SLA credits: 5% credit per 0.1% below guarantee, capped at 30%
- Credit requests must be submitted within 30 days of the incident

## Data Retention

- Active accounts: Data retained indefinitely
- Cancelled accounts: Data retained for 90 days after cancellation, then permanently deleted
- Deleted records: Moved to trash, retained for 30 days, then permanently purged
- Audit logs: Retained for 1 year (Enterprise) or 90 days (other plans)

## Escalation Path

If a customer is dissatisfied with their support experience:
1. Level 1: Support agent resolves common issues using the knowledge base
2. Level 2: Senior support engineer for technical deep-dives
3. Level 3: Engineering team for bugs or infrastructure issues
4. Level 4: Account manager + VP of Customer Success for relationship issues

Escalation to Level 2+ triggers automatic notification to the customer's account manager (Enterprise) or sends a satisfaction survey (all plans).
