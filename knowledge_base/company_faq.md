## Account Management

### How do I reset my password?
1. Go to app.novacrm.com/login
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. You'll receive a reset link within 2 minutes
5. The link expires after 1 hour

If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

### How do I change my email address?
Go to Settings → Profile → Email. You'll need to verify the new email address before the change takes effect. Note: only account administrators can change emails for other team members.

### How do I add team members?
Navigate to Settings → Team → Invite Members. Enter their email addresses. They will receive an invitation to join your workspace. Each new member counts toward your per-user billing.

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Go to Settings → Billing → Change Plan. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. If upgrading, you'll get immediate access to new features with a prorated charge.

## Technical Support

### My dashboard is loading slowly
This is usually caused by one of these issues:
- Browser cache: Clear your browser cache and cookies, then reload
- Large dataset: If you have over 10,000 contacts, try using filters to narrow your view
- Browser extensions: Ad blockers or VPN extensions can slow performance
- Network: Check your internet speed at speedtest.net (we recommend at least 10 Mbps)

If the issue persists after trying these steps, contact support with your browser version and a screenshot.

### I can't send emails from NovaCRM
Verify these settings:
1. Go to Settings → Integrations → Email
2. Ensure your email provider (Gmail/Outlook) is connected and authorized
3. Check that your daily send limit hasn't been exceeded (Starter: 200/day, Professional: 1,000/day, Enterprise: 5,000/day)
4. Make sure your email isn't flagged for spam — check your sender reputation

### API rate limits
- Starter: 100 requests/hour
- Professional: 1,000 requests/hour
- Enterprise: 10,000 requests/hour
- Webhook events: All plans receive real-time webhook notifications

### Data export
You can export your data at any time from Settings → Data → Export. Supported formats: CSV, JSON, and Excel. Full exports typically complete within 15 minutes for accounts under 50,000 contacts.
